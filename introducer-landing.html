Is there any cost to become an introducer?

No. It's 100% free. No signup fees, no monthly fees, no hidden costs. We make money when you successfully help people, not from recruiting you.

How do I get paid?

Via Stripe Connect (same system Uber/Airbnb use). Payouts happen automatically on the 5th of each month for the previous month's commissions. Minimum payout is ¬£10.

Do I need to be an existing customer?

No requirement, but we recommend trying the demo first so you understand what you're sharing. Authenticity matters - it's easier to recommend something you believe in.

What if someone cancels their subscription?

You stop earning commission for that customer. This keeps the system honest - you're incentivized to only refer people who will genuinely benefit long-term.

Is this an MLM or pyramid scheme?

No. You earn from customers you refer directly (¬£5.85/month). There's no requirement to recruit other introducers. Simple, transparent, ethical.

Can I share this in multiple communities?

Yes! Many of our top introducers manage multiple Facebook groups or forums. As long as you're genuinely helping people, share away.
